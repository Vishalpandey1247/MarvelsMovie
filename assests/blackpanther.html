BLACK PANTHER

T'Challa now prepares to be proclaimed new king of Wakanda after his father's death. T'Challa, however, finds that his position is now being threatened by the arrival of an old adversary called Killmonger, which endangers both Wakanda and the planet. Teaming with the Dora Milaje, his little sister Shuri and his CIA ally Everett K. Ross, T'Challa must harness the  Black Panther's forces to fight against his enemy and save Wakanda from destruction.
