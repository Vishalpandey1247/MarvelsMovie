AVENGERS


It is 1941 and the world is in the throes of war. Steve Rogers wants to serve his part for country and join America's armed forces, but the military rejects him because of his unfit body and small stature. Finally, Steve gets his chance to serve his country when he is accepted into an experimental program that turns him into a super soldier.
